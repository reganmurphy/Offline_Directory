Our Book Recs



Books have always been an excellent source of inspiration, entertainment, and knowledge providing a way to disconnect from the outside world. Whether you enjoy fiction or nonfiction, there's no doubt that a great book has the power to transport us on a journey, extend our minds, and inspire us in numerous ways. That is why we created a list of our favourite books!

  • To Kill a Mockingbird by Harper Lee (1960)
  • 1984 George Orwell (1949)
  • A Wrinkle In Time L’Engle (1962)
  • Harry Potter (All books)
  • The Giver by Lois Lowry (1993)
  • The Great Gatsby by F. Scott Fitzgerald (1925)
  • Little Women by Louisa May Alcott (1868)
  • The Lion, the Witch, and the Wardrobe by C.S. Lewis (1950)
  • The Kite Runner by Khaled Hosseini (2003)
  • Alice’s Adventures in Wonderland and Through the Looking-Glass by Lewis Carroll (1865)
  • The Secret Garden by Frances Hodgson Burnett
