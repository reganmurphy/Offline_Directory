About us!

Welcome! We believe there is enjoyment to be experienced off of the internet! With the internet being a part of our everyday lives now it's hard to imagine even a day without our phones, laptops, and other devices that keep us connected. It’s crucial to get away from our screens frequently for our mental and physical health and to experience the real world. You can find a tonne of inspiration and ideas for things to do that don't require an internet connection right here! So you can learn to put your laptop or phone down, and learn how to live offline (sometimes)!
